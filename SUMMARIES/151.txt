The shou talks to a woman who thought she was going to die in quicksand. She describes the moment she realized she was sinking in the sand at a beach in Massachusetts. She also talks about how she tried to pull her feet out of the sand to save herself. The shou is a weekly, offbeat look at the world through the eyes of an American woman.
